Scoring Methodology

SDG scores are calculated using a standardized methodology that ensures comparability across countries and goals:

  • Normalization: All indicators are normalized to a 0-100 scale where 100 represents SDG achievement
  • Aggregation: Goal-level scores aggregate indicator values using equal weights
  • Country Scores: Overall country scores are the arithmetic mean of all 17 goal scores
  • Trend Analysis: Progress trends are calculated using compound annual growth rates
  • Missing Data: Countries with less than 80% indicator coverage are excluded from rankings
  • Regional Grouping: Based on UN geographical classifications
