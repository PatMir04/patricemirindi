About Patrice Mirindi

Transforming data into sustainable impact across emerging markets

My Story

I am Patrice Lekeraho Mirindi, a Senior Data Analyst & Economic Development Consultant with a proven track record of delivering data-driven solutions that shape policies, drive investment, and foster sustainable economic growth in emerging markets.

Originally from the Democratic Republic of Congo, I bring a unique perspective that combines deep understanding of emerging market challenges with advanced analytical skills. My journey has taken me across continents, working with international organizations like the World Bank, GIZ, and the Financial Resilience Institute.

With extensive expertise in economic policy, market intelligence, financial forecasting, and impact evaluation, I help governments, international organizations, and businesses make informed decisions that create lasting change. My unique on-the-ground experience combined with advanced data analytics enables me to bridge the gap between policy frameworks and real-world implementation.

My Journey

Educational Foundation

MSc Agricultural Economics - University of Nairobi

Research focused on agricultural market dynamics and economic policy for sustainable development, with emphasis on food security and rural development strategies in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Early Career

Entrepreneur Support & Portfolio Manager - The Anzisha Prize

Started my professional journey supporting young African entrepreneurs, where I developed passion for using data to drive economic empowerment and sustainable business growth.

Consulting Excellence

Business Development Consultant - Economic Sense

Expanded into international consulting, working across multiple countries and developing expertise in financial modeling, market research, and strategic business development.

Current Role

Senior Data Analyst - Financial Resilience Institute

Now leading complex data analyses and strategic consulting to advance financial resilience across Canada and globally, bringing my international experience to new markets.
