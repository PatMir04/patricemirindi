About Me

Passionate about transforming data into sustainable impact

Transforming Data into Sustainable Impact

I'm Patrice Mirindi, a Senior Data Analyst and Economic Development Consultant with over 8 years of experience turning complex data into actionable insights that drive sustainable growth across emerging markets.

Currently serving as a Senior Data Analyst at the Financial Resilience Institute in Vancouver, Canada, I specialize in financial resilience frameworks, agricultural economics, and data-driven policy analysis. My work spans 12+ countries, impacting over 2,000 lives through evidence-based interventions.

I believe that data has the power to transform communities, accelerate business potential, and reshape the future of emerging markets. Through rigorous analysis, strategic consulting, and collaborative partnerships, I help organizations make informed decisions that create lasting positive impact.
