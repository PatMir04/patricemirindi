My Journey: From Kinshasa to Global Development

A passion for transforming communities through evidence-based solutions

Roots in the Democratic Republic of Congo

Born and raised in the Democratic Republic of Congo, I witnessed firsthand the challenges and opportunities that define developing economies. Growing up in Kinshasa, I experienced the daily realities of economic uncertainty, agricultural struggles, and the persistent hope for sustainable development that characterizes much of Sub-Saharan Africa.

These early experiences ignited my passion for understanding how data and evidence-based policy could transform communities. I saw the gap between academic theory and ground-level reality, which would later shape my approach to development consulting—always grounded in real-world context and community needs.

Academic Foundation in Nairobi

My pursuit of academic excellence led me to the University of Nairobi, where I earned my Master of Science in Agricultural Economics. This program provided the perfect intersection of my interests: understanding agricultural systems, economic policy, and data-driven decision making.

At the University of Nairobi, I specialized in agricultural value chains, market analysis, and policy evaluation. My research focused on smallholder farmer resilience and the role of data analytics in improving agricultural productivity across East Africa. This academic grounding became the foundation for my later work across multiple countries and contexts.

From Theory to Global Practice

Armed with academic credentials and a deep understanding of African economic challenges, I began working across multiple countries, applying my knowledge to real-world development projects. From South Africa to Canada, from agricultural value chains in Ghana to financial resilience frameworks in Winnipeg, my career has been defined by the pursuit of sustainable, data-driven impact.

What sets my approach apart is the combination of lived experience in developing economies, rigorous academic training, and extensive field practice. I don't just analyze data—I understand the human stories behind the numbers and the cultural contexts that shape economic outcomes.

Personal Mission & Values

My mission is simple: transform complex data into actionable strategies that create lasting positive impact. Whether working with international organizations, government agencies, or private sector clients, I bring a unique perspective shaped by my journey from the DRC to global development practice.

I believe that the most effective development solutions emerge when rigorous data analysis meets deep cultural understanding and genuine community engagement. My multilingual abilities—French, English, Swahili, and Lingala—allow me to work effectively across diverse cultural contexts, building trust and delivering solutions that truly resonate with local communities.

Core Values & Professional Approach

The principles that guide my work and client relationships

Evidence-Based Decision Making

Every recommendation I provide is grounded in rigorous data analysis and empirical evidence. I believe that sustainable development requires solutions backed by solid research and measurable outcomes.

Community-Centered Development

Drawing from my experiences in the DRC and across Africa, I ensure that all projects prioritize community needs and local context. True impact comes from solutions that resonate with the people they're designed to serve.

Collaborative Partnership

I work as a strategic partner, not just a consultant. My multilingual abilities and cross-cultural experience enable me to build strong relationships with stakeholders across diverse contexts and backgrounds.

Sustainable Impact Focus

Every project is designed with long-term sustainability in mind. I focus on building local capacity, transferring knowledge, and creating systems that continue to generate value long after project completion.

Cultural Intelligence

My journey from the DRC to global practice has taught me the importance of cultural context in development work. I adapt my methodologies to respect local customs while delivering international best practices.

Continuous Innovation

I constantly seek new analytical techniques, tools, and approaches to solve complex development challenges. My work bridges traditional economic analysis with cutting-edge data science methodologies.
