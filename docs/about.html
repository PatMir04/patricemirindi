Roots in the Democratic Republic of Congo

Born and raised in the Democratic Republic of Congo, I witnessed firsthand the challenges and opportunities that define developing economies. Growing up in DRC, I experienced the daily realities of economic uncertainty, agricultural struggles, and the persistent hope for sustainable development that characterizes much of Sub-Saharan Africa.

These early experiences ignited my passion for understanding how data and evidence-based policy could transform communities. I saw the gap between academic theory and ground-level reality, which would later shape my approach to development consulting, always grounded in real-world context and community needs.

Core Values & Professional Approach

The principles that guide my work and client relationships

Evidence-Based Decision Making

Every recommendation I provide is grounded in rigorous data analysis and empirical evidence. I believe that sustainable development requires solutions backed by solid research and measurable outcomes.

Community-Centered Development

Drawing from my experiences in the DRC and across Africa, I ensure that all projects prioritize community needs and local context. True impact comes from solutions that resonate with the people they're designed to serve.

Collaborative Partnership

I work as a strategic partner, not just a consultant. My multilingual abilities and cross-cultural experience enable me to build strong relationships with stakeholders across diverse contexts and backgrounds.

Sustainable Impact Focus

Every project is designed with long-term sustainability in mind. I focus on building local capacity, transferring knowledge, and creating systems that continue to generate value long after project completion.

Cultural Intelligence

My journey from the DRC to global practice has taught me the importance of cultural context in development work. I adapt my methodologies to respect local customs while delivering international best practices.

Continuous Innovation

I constantly seek new analytical techniques, tools, and approaches to solve complex development challenges. My work bridges traditional economic analysis with cutting-edge data science methodologies.
