My Story

I am a Senior Data Analyst & Economic Development Consultant with a proven track record of delivering data-driven solutions that shape policies, drive investment, and foster sustainable economic growth in emerging markets, particularly in Africa.

With extensive expertise in economic policy, market intelligence, financial forecasting, and impact evaluation, I help governments, international organizations, and businesses make informed decisions that create lasting change. My unique on-the-ground experience combined with advanced data analytics enables me to bridge the gap between policy frameworks and real-world implementation.

Whether advising governments, NGOs, or private institutions, I combine analytics, strategic foresight, and a passion for inclusive development to drive resilient outcomes that benefit communities and accelerate sustainable growth.
