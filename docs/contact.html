Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in data analytics and economic development projects including:

  • Financial resilience assessments and framework development
  • Agricultural value chain analysis and market research
  • Economic policy analysis and impact evaluation
  • Statistical modeling and data visualization
  • International development project management
  • Capacity building and training programs

What are your typical project timelines and rates?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity:

  • Data analysis projects: 2-8 weeks depending on dataset size and complexity
  • Policy research: 4-12 weeks for comprehensive analysis
  • Impact evaluations: 3-6 months including data collection and analysis
  • Long-term consulting: 6-18 months for major initiatives

Rates are project-specific and depend on scope, timeline, and deliverables. I offer competitive rates and provide detailed proposals with transparent pricing. Contact me for a customized quote.

Do you work with international clients and organizations?

Yes, I have extensive experience working with international clients across 12+ countries. I'm comfortable working across different time zones and cultural contexts. My experience includes:

  • International development organizations (World Bank, GIZ, EU)
  • Government agencies and ministries
  • Private sector companies and consulting firms
  • NGOs and foundations
  • Academic institutions and research centers

What tools and software do you use?

I'm proficient in a comprehensive suite of analytical and visualization tools:

  • Statistical Analysis: R, Python, Stata, SPSS
  • Data Visualization: Power BI, Tableau, Excel, ggplot2
  • Database Management: SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL
  • Survey & Geographic: KoBo Toolbox, QGIS, SurveyCTO
  • Project Management: Microsoft Project, Asana, Teams

I adapt my tool selection based on client preferences and project requirements.

How do you ensure project quality and deliverable standards?

I maintain high quality standards through:

  • Rigorous methodology: Evidence-based approaches and peer-reviewed methods
  • Quality assurance: Multiple review cycles and validation processes
  • Clear communication: Regular progress updates and stakeholder feedback
  • Professional standards: PMP certification and continuous professional development
  • Documentation: Comprehensive reporting and reproducible analysis

Do you offer training and capacity building services?

Yes, I provide comprehensive training and capacity building services including:

  • Data analysis and statistical methods training
  • R and Python programming workshops
  • Economic analysis and research methodology
  • Impact evaluation and monitoring & evaluation systems
  • Dashboard development and data visualization
  • Custom training programs tailored to organizational needs

Training can be delivered remotely or on-site, with materials provided in multiple languages.
