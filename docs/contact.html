Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in data analytics, financial resilience consulting, economic development strategy, and project management. My projects range from market research and policy analysis to dashboard development and capacity building programs.

Do you work with both organizations and governments?

Yes, I work with a diverse range of clients including international organizations (World Bank, EU, GIZ), non-profits, private companies, and government agencies across multiple countries.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Small analysis projects may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive multi-country initiatives can span 6-18 months. I always provide realistic timelines during the proposal phase.

Do you offer training and capacity building services?

Absolutely! I provide customized training in data analytics, economic research methodologies, project management, and statistical analysis. Training can be delivered in-person or virtually.

Can you work remotely with international clients?

Yes, I frequently collaborate with international clients remotely. I'm experienced in working across different time zones and have established processes for effective virtual collaboration and project delivery.

What makes your approach different?

I combine advanced technical skills with deep understanding of emerging market contexts. My multicultural experience and multilingual capabilities enable me to bridge cultural gaps and deliver solutions that are both analytically rigorous and practically implementable.
