EU Trade Facilitation Program

European Union & Economic Sense

International Trade

Analyzed trade barriers and opportunities for African agricultural exports to EU markets, developing comprehensive trade facilitation strategies. The project focused on regulatory compliance, logistical efficiency, and market access for African exporters.

Project Objectives:
  • Identify regulatory and logistical trade barriers facing African exporters
  • Develop market entry strategies for agricultural products
  • Create comprehensive trade facilitation frameworks

Outcomes & Impact:

  • Trained 150+ exporters on EU compliance requirements
  • Established sustainable trade partnerships
